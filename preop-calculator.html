📖 About Preoperative Risk Assessment

The Pre-operative Risk Calculator is designed to help clinicians assess the risk of postoperative pulmonary complications in surgical patients. This tool integrates multiple clinical parameters including lung function, exercise capacity, and patient demographics to provide a comprehensive risk stratification model that supports informed surgical decision-making and perioperative optimization.
