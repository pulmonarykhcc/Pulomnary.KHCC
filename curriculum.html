Program at a Glance

Fellowship Overview

The fellowship spans 3 academic years (July 1 – June 30), structured as 52 weekly blocks per year. First-year fellows begin with an intensive 8-week orientation on the Pulmonary Service before proceeding to other rotations.

What You'll Experience

Rotation Highlights

🫁
Pulmonary Service
78 blocks · Core of the program
Inpatient consults, outpatient clinics, bronchoscopy, interventional pulmonology, PFT lab, and smoking cessation clinics. Clinics and procedures run throughout the year, not only during dedicated rotation blocks.
🏥
Medical ICU
10 blocks · Critical care immersion
Year 1 fellows serve as 1st on-call; Year 2 as 2nd on-call. Covers ARDS, ECMO, mechanical ventilation, vasopressor management, and multidisciplinary critical care in a high-acuity environment.
😴
Sleep Medicine
12 blocks · 4 weeks per year
Polysomnography interpretation, OSA/OHS/central apnea management, CPAP/BiPAP titration, and sleep-related breathing disorder clinics. Dedicated sleep lab exposure throughout all 3 years.
✂️
Thoracic Surgery
10 blocks · Surgical perspective
Operating room exposure, post-thoracotomy care, preoperative pulmonary evaluation, chest wall reconstruction, and combined surgical-pulmonary MDC participation.
🔬
Thoracic Radiology
10 blocks · Imaging mastery
Advanced interpretation of chest CT, high-resolution CT for diffuse lung disease, CXR, PET-CT, V/Q scans, and CT-bronchoscopy anatomical correlation with dedicated thoracic radiologists.
📊
Research
7 blocks · Yr 2 & 3 only
Dedicated research time with faculty mentorship. At least one completed research project is a requirement for graduation. Elective rotations can include additional research time.

Program Structure Rules

Key Schedule Features

🏁
Year 1 Orientation
Both first-year fellows (F1 & F2) begin with a minimum 8-week Pulmonary Service orientation starting July 1, establishing foundational clinical competencies before other rotations.
📞
On-Call Structure
Year 1 fellows serve as 1st on-call; Year 2 fellows as 2nd on-call. At least one Year 1 fellow must be on Pulmonary Service alongside one senior fellow at all times.
🔁
Rotation Flexibility
Rotations longer than 4 blocks can be split across the year. Shorter rotations (<4 blocks) may be combined within a single month for efficiency and continuity of learning.
🎓
Research Requirement
Completion of at least one research project is a mandatory graduation requirement. Elective rotations can be used for additional dedicated research time with faculty mentorship.
🌐
Elective Flexibility
Elective rotations can be completed at KHCC (home) or at external institutions nationally and internationally, allowing fellows to pursue specific subspecialty interests.
🏖️
Vacation Policy
3 vacation blocks (weeks) per year. At least 2 of the 3 vacation blocks must be scheduled in advance through the program coordinator to ensure coverage.
