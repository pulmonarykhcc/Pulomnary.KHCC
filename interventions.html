Procedural Training

Core Procedures & Interventions

Hover over each card to learn more. Our fellows achieve genuine competence — not just exposure — in every procedure listed below.

Bronchoscopy
🔭
Advanced Bronchoscopy & Interventional Pulmonology
Diagnostic and therapeutic bronchoscopy, EBUS-TBNA, endobronchial stenting, foreign body removal, and complex airway interventions. Dedicated IP rotation plus ongoing procedural exposure throughout the year.
POCUS
📡
Thoracic POCUS & Echocardiography
Point-of-care lung and cardiac ultrasound for real-time diagnosis, procedure guidance, and hemodynamic monitoring. Bi-monthly simulation sessions plus dedicated POCUS rotation blocks.
Sleep Medicine
😴
Polysomnography & Sleep Medicine
Full polysomnogram setup and interpretation, PAP titration, management of OSA, OHS, central sleep apnea, and complex sleep-disordered breathing. 4 weeks per year across all 3 training years.
Critical Care
🫁
Complex Airway Management & Tracheostomy
Advanced airway techniques, percutaneous and surgical tracheostomy, difficult airway scenarios, and management of patients requiring long-term ventilatory support in the MICU and beyond.
Pleural
🩺
Chest Tube Placement & Thoracoscopy
Thoracentesis, chest tube insertion for pneumothorax and pleural effusion, medical thoracoscopy with biopsy, pleurodesis, and indwelling pleural catheter management.
ECMO
⚡
ECMO & Advanced Critical Care
Management of extracorporeal membrane oxygenation, ARDS, and refractory respiratory failure in close collaboration with the MICU and cardiothoracic teams at KHCC.
PFT Lab
📈
Pulmonary Function Testing & CPET
Complete spirometry, static lung volumes, DLCO measurement, bronchoprovocation (methacholine/mannitol challenge), inhalational challenge testing, and cardiopulmonary exercise testing interpretation.
Vascular
❤️
Right Heart Catheterization
Hemodynamic assessment for pulmonary arterial hypertension diagnosis, vasoreactivity testing, and catheter-based monitoring. Training in right-sided waveform interpretation and PHTN management.
Radiology
🩻
Thoracic Imaging & CT Correlation
Advanced interpretation of chest CT, high-resolution CT for diffuse lung disease, CXR, PET-CT, V/Q scan, and systematic CT-bronchoscopy anatomical correlation with dedicated thoracic radiologists.

Simulation-Based Procedural Training

Supervised patient care and simulation are the twin pillars of procedural education at KHCC. Fellows build genuine competence through stepwise progression — from task trainer to supervised clinical performance to independent practice.

Bi-monthly simulation sessions supervised by Dr. Naser Mahmoud cover a comprehensive range of clinical scenarios and technical skills.
