📖 BODE Index Staging

The BODE Index score ranges from 0-10, with higher scores indicating worse prognosis. Each component contributes points based on severity.

Stage I (0-2)

Low risk

Stage II (3-4)

Moderate risk

Stage III (5-6)

High risk

Stage IV (7-10)

Very high risk
