📖 About PE Risk Assessment

The PE Risk Calculator is a comprehensive tool that helps clinicians assess the probability of pulmonary embolism based on clinical findings, imaging results, and laboratory parameters. This tool supports clinical decision-making in the diagnosis and management of suspected PE.
