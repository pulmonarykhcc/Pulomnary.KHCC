📖 About CURB-65

The CURB-65 score assigns 1 point for each of the following: Confusion, Urea > 7 mmol/L (BUN > 19 mg/dL), Respiratory rate ≥ 30/min, Blood pressure abnormality (Systolic < 90 or Diastolic ≤ 60 mmHg), and Age ≥ 65. Total score ranges from 0 to 5, with higher scores indicating increased risk of mortality and need for hospitalization or ICU care.
