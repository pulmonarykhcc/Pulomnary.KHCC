Why Train at KHCC?

King Hussein Cancer Center stands as Jordan's premier oncology and specialty care institution. Our Pulmonary Medicine Fellowship offers an unparalleled training environment where fellows encounter high-acuity pulmonary disease in a dedicated academic setting.

From advanced interventional bronchoscopy and EBUS to complex ILD, pulmonary HTN, and Airway diseases and management — our program prepares you to be a complete, confident pulmonologist ready to lead respiratory care in the region.
