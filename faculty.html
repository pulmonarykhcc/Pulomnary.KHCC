Fellowship Structure for 6 Fellows

The program accommodates 6 fellows across 3 year cohorts (F1–F6). Fellows F1 & F2 are in Year 1, F3 & F4 in Year 2, and F5 & F6 in Year 3. This structure ensures that at least one first-year fellow is always on Pulmonary Service alongside a senior fellow, maintaining continuity of service and mentorship.

🏁
F1 & F2 — Year 1
Begin with 8-week Pulmonary Service orientation. Serve as 1st on-call for the program.
📈
F3 & F4 — Year 2
Progress to 2nd on-call. Begin research and elective blocks. Serve as senior mentors to Year 1.
🎓
F5 & F6 — Year 3
Maximum elective flexibility (8 blocks). Research completion. Leadership and teaching roles within the program.
✅
Graduation Requirement
At least one completed and accepted research project required for graduation from the fellowship program.
