Structured Learning

Academic Activities

Our academic calendar is carefully designed to ensure comprehensive coverage of pulmonary medicine through multiple complementary formats — from bedside teaching to simulation labs.

🎓
Core Lectures
📅 Every Thursday · 8:00 – 9:00 AM
Structured didactic lectures covering the full breadth of pulmonary medicine. Presented by attending consultants or senior fellows. Topics follow the annual didactic schedule with dedicated faculty supervision.
🩺
Chest Conference / Case Conference
📅 Every Wednesday · 1:00 – 2:00 PM
Weekly case-based conference where fellows and faculty bring challenging, interesting, or educational cases from the past week for group discussion, differential diagnosis, and learning.
🏥
ICU Multidisciplinary Activity
📅 Every Wednesday · 8:00 – 9:00 AM
Combined critical care educational activity with Pharmacy, Nursing, Respiratory Therapy, and allied health teams. Topics selected to avoid overlap with other sessions, emphasizing team-based critical care.
🏫
JUH Activity
📅 Every Tuesday (except 4th) · 8:00 – 9:00 AM
Weekly academic collaboration with Jordan University Hospital. The 4th Tuesday of each month is reserved for Journal Club or Case Presentation instead.
💻
Lung Multidisciplinary Conference (MDC)
📅 Every Wednesday · 2:00 – 3:00 PM · via Zoom
Virtual lung tumor board and multidisciplinary conference uniting pulmonology, thoracic oncology, radiology, and thoracic surgery teams for complex case discussion and treatment planning.
📝
SEEK Board Review & OSCE
📆 Last Sunday of each month · 8:00 AM
Monthly board-style review focusing on a specific pulmonary topic, paired with an OSCE-style assessment. Supervised by Dr. Naser Mahmoud. Designed to prepare fellows for board examinations while reinforcing clinical knowledge.
🗂️
Flea Market
📆 Quarterly · 1st Monday of Sep, Dec, Mar, Jun · 8–9 AM
Quarterly review of interesting and educational ABGs, CT scans, CXRs, polysomnograms, PFTs, and bronchoscopy findings. Presented by a fellow for group analysis and learning. A favorite among trainees.
📖
Journal Club / Case Presentations
📆 4th Tuesday · Alternating months (Sep, Nov, Jan, Mar, May, Jul) · 8–9 AM
Alternating monthly format — one month is dedicated to journal club (critical appraisal of recent literature), the next to case presentations. Led by a fellow, supervised by a consultant.
📋
Guidelines Review
📆 Every 2 months · 1st or 2nd Thursday · 8–9 AM
Systematic review and summarization of the latest international pulmonary guidelines — GINA, GOLD, IDSA, ATS, and others. Presented by a fellow and supervised by a consultant faculty member.
🎯
Lung POCUS & Simulation
📆 Bi-monthly · Mondays · 8:00 – 9:00 AM · Supervised by Dr. Naser
Hands-on simulation sessions covering POCUS, endotracheal intubation, inhalational challenge (PFT), diagnostic and therapeutic bronchoscopy, chest tube placement, percutaneous tracheostomy, ECMO, EBUS, CPET, sleep studies, and other clinical scenarios.
🤝
Monthly Combined Conferences
📆 Last Thursday of each month · 2:00 – 4:00 PM
A rotating series of multidisciplinary conferences in collaboration with KHCC departments and external institutions. Each conference brings a different specialty perspective to pulmonary medicine. Two additional slots are available for annual conferences (CPC, Sleep, ID, IP, Bronchoscopy, or outside consultants).
