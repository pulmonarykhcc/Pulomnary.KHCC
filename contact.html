How to Apply

Application Process

1
Check Eligibility
Candidates must hold an MD degree and have completed at least 4 years of Internal Medicine residency training at an accredited institution and JMC board certified.
2
Prepare Documents
Gather your CV, medical degree and residency certificates, board exam scores, 3 letters of recommendation, and a personal statement.
3
Submit Inquiry
Complete the inquiry form above or email directly to the program. Our team will guide you through the next steps and provide the official application link (usually in Apr of each year).
4
Interview
Shortlisted candidates after a written exam are invited for an in-person interview with the Program Director and faculty committee.
5
Decision & Offer
Accepted candidates receive a formal offer letter. The fellowship start date is July 1 of each academic year.
6
Join Our Program
Begin your journey at KHCC with an 8-week Pulmonary Service orientation alongside your co-fellows and faculty mentors.
